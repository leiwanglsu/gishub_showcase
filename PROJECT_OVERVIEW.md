# Project Overview

## Objective
GIS Hub is designed to convert mobile LiDAR capture workflows into structured, auditable, and repeatable elevation intelligence for screening and pilot operations.

## Product Direction
The commercialization path emphasizes:
- Operational usability for municipal and program stakeholders
- Reproducible outputs with clear governance boundaries
- Transition readiness from pilot screening to certification-aligned workflows

## Current Product Posture
- Pilot-ready for preliminary screening workflows
- Non-certification by design in current phase
- Certification-final outputs deferred until control/checkpoint evidence and licensed review are available

## Companion Product Note
A companion iOS LiDAR capture application (BOR PoCP, iPad/iPhone) is under active assessment as a parallel field-capture asset.
