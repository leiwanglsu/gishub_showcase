# Risk and Governance (Public Summary)

## Governance Principle
All current outputs are preliminary and non-certification.

## Primary Risk Categories
- Evidence completeness risk (control/checkpoint dependencies)
- Interpretation risk (screening results mistaken for certification)
- Operational consistency risk across deployments

## Mitigation Posture
- Explicit readiness states
- Non-certification communication controls
- Staged gates before external-facing handoff
