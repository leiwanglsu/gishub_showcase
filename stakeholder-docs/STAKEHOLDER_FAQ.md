# Stakeholder FAQ

## Is this system certification-ready today?
No. The current phase is pilot-ready for preliminary screening and planning workflows.

## Can municipalities use outputs for final elevation certificates?
No. Final certificate use requires control/checkpoint evidence and licensed professional review.

## What does "readiness state" mean?
It is the project status indicator used to communicate quality posture and dependency completeness in a controlled, auditable way.

## Why are governance controls emphasized?
Because pilot outputs can appear persuasive; governance controls prevent over-interpretation and protect stakeholder decision quality.

## What is planned next?
Control/checkpoint integration, expanded validation, and product hardening for commercialization readiness.

## Where does the companion iOS app fit?
It is a companion capture asset under assessment. Integration is intentionally deferred until later-phase planning.
